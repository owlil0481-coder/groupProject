Aramco Company

Aramco is a Saudi Arabian company that works in the field of oil and gas. It was founded in 1933 and has grown to become one of the largest and most successful companies in the world.

Main Goals

  • Develop and manage energy resources efficiently.
  • Support the Saudi economy and contribute to national growth.
  • Invest in technology and innovation for the energy sector.
  • Protect the environment and promote sustainable practices.

Achievements

  • Owns one of the largest proven oil reserves in the world.
  • Produces millions of barrels of oil every day.
  • Recognized globally as one of the most profitable companies.

Future Vision

  • Contribute to Saudi Vision 2030 by supporting renewable energy projects.
  • Expand in international markets and improve global partnerships.
  • Promote education and training in the energy and technology sectors.
